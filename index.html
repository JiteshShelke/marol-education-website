Campus Facilities & Student Resources

Library & Reading Room

The School Library & Reading Room has a wide collection of books in English, Hindi and Marathi. Newspapers, magazines and periodicals for children are also subscribed.

The Library has two sections – one for students with academic resources, and a General Section with fiction & non-fiction books for parents, alumni, and staff. A full-time librarian manages the library, guided by a senior teacher. The Library is open on all working days from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.

E-Learning

Since 2012, we have integrated digital learning with smart boards, making classrooms interactive and engaging. TATA ClassEdge provides an advanced educational interface that enriches traditional teaching.

This blend of conventional and digital learning helps teachers bring vast resources into classrooms, making the learning process more exciting while keeping the teacher central to the experience.

School Bus

The School Transport Committee, led by the Principal, ensures safe and efficient school transport. Routes are planned for minimal travel time, with strict compliance to RTO & Education Department norms.

The committee also oversees bus maintenance, driver/attendant conduct, and fare structures to maintain a no profit – no loss service.

Sports

The Sports Committee, led by the Principal, plans and supervises sports activities. Coaches and PE Instructors guide students in games such as badminton, basketball, football, and athletics.

The committee prepares the inter-house sports calendar in line with state-level competitions, and also considers parent suggestions for continuous improvement of sports activities.
